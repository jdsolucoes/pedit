What is pedit?.

Pedit is a simple editor which aims to solve a problem that many programmers encounter during their development cycles, the problem is to type the commit message, all standard editors are simply ineffective or are too complex (I'm looking at you VIM) or simply boring . Anyway, pedit was born with this noble purpose, to free developers from having to type ESC + wq! + RETURN.
